Service Models

IaaS vs PaaS vs SaaS

Service Models — Examples

IaaS

Like renting a bare apartment — walls and plumbing, you furnish it

e.g. Hetzner Cloud, AWS EC2, Azure VMs

PaaS

Like a co-working space — desk, WiFi, coffee provided, bring your laptop

e.g. Heroku, Vercel, Netlify

SaaS

Like a hotel — everything ready, just show up

e.g. Google Docs, Spotify, ChatGPT

The service model maps to migration strategies later: IaaS → Rehost (lift and shift) PaaS → Replatform (use managed services) SaaS → Repurchase (replace with SaaS alternative)
