What is Cloud Sovereignty?

Data Sovereignty

Data is subject to the laws of the country where it is stored

Operational Sovereignty

Control over operations — no foreign administrative access

Software Sovereignty

Independence from vendor lock-in and proprietary platforms

Digital Sovereignty

Strategic autonomy over digital infrastructure and data

Sovereignty Takeaways

  • Data residency alone is not sovereignty
  • Sovereignty requirements impact provider, region, and service choices
  • Key management is critical: BYOK, HYOK, or external KMS
  • Swiss regulations (nDSG, FINMA) increasingly demand sovereignty controls
