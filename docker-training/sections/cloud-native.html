Cloud Native

What is Cloud Native?

Cloud native practices empower organizations to develop, build, and deploy workloads in computing environments (public, private, hybrid cloud) to meet their organizational needs at scale in a programmatic and repeatable manner. These are characterized by loosely coupled systems that interoperate in a manner that is secure, resilient, manageable, sustainable, and observable.

Cloud native technologies typically consist of some combination of containers, service meshes, multi-tenancy, microservices, immutable infrastructure, serverless, and declarative APIs. Combined with robust automation, they enable organizations to make high-impact changes frequently, predictably, with minimal toil and clear separation of concerns.
